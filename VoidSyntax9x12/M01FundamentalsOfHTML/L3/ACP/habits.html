Daily Healthy Habits

Top 5 Healthy Foods

  • Leafy greens
  • Fruits (e.g. apples, bananas)
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Whole grains
  • Yogurt

Steps to Start Your Day Right

  1. Wake up early
  2. Drink a glass of water
  3. Stretch or exercise
  4. Eat a balanced breakfast
  5. Plan your goals for the day

Key Concepts

Hydration
Drinking enough water throughout the day.
Balanced Diet
A mix of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, and vitamins.
Exercise
Any physical activity that keeps your body fit.
